God has 5 methods that he uses to communicate with us
1) The Word - the written word.
2) The Spoken Word - what we get from preaching, prophetic speaking, and song.
3) Prayer - our quite spaces, where we choose to commune and dialog with God.  I stress quiet, because it also requires us to shut our mouths and receive.
4) The Fellowship of Christian Friends - God works through His people and will use others to bring us a word when we're not getting it directly from Him.
5) Circumstances in our lives.

The point that Pastor Mark was making to us was simple.  If you are not reading your Word, if you're not coming to church and hearing the message, receiving the prophetic word, and listening to the songs, if you are not spending time in prayer and reflection, and if you're not fellowshipping with you Christian community, you're leaving God only one area to communicate with you and draw you back to Him.  That's the one place you don't really want God trying to get your attention; that's through circumstances in your life.
